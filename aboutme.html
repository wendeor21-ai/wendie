About Me

Hi, I'm Wendwesen Wagaw

I was born on August 11, 2002 G.C. in Sekota Woreda, located in the Wag Hemra Zone of the Amhara Region, Ethiopia. My father is Wagaw Alemu and my mother is Muluwerk Mengste. I am proud to have a large and supportive family, including four sisters and two brothers.

From a young age, I have been fascinated by computers and technology. Today, I am a 3rd-year Information Systems undergraduate student at the University of Gondar. My dream is to become a skilled Full-Stack Developer capable of creating modern, user-friendly digital solutions.
